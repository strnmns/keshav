My reading list for 2023

Never have I ever had a reading list. I see it as something that's pointless. You face a problem or you want to learn something new, you pick up a book or two. As simple as that.

I read 14 books in 2022. But to my surprise, most of them were sci-fi. Turns out that when I'm curious, I'd rather read an article or listen to an hour-long podcast rather than pick up a book to satisfy my curiosity. "YAY! SAVED TIME!"... only one slight problem, I never get any in-depth knowledge about anything.

But this year I want to read exclusively about business and not let my curiosity decide what I should read — and I know putting a brake on one's curiosity for knowing how stuff works is a fool's errand, and I'd venture a more robust explanation as to why, but sticking with the spirit of this post, I'll save my theorizing.
